About Me

❖

Football

Since I was six years old I joined a football team. And still play to this very day. The thrill of the game and the potential to achieve certain goals while working with a team is very satisfiying. Also the strong friendships that emerge from team sports are precious.

Technology

Technology is the future. It will advance humankind, it brought us to the moon and back. It marvels me how much a cellphone is capable of doing. And with coding I can be part of this. Creating a project that could help or inform someone. The ability to create gives the ability to change.

Wanderlust

Exploring the many places our world has to offer really feeds my wanderlust. The different cultures and natural phenomenals made my frequent travels very memorable. From walking to Santiago de Compostella, or traveling very local in Albania and Iceland. While working in countries such as South-Africa, Sri Lanka, Mauritius and New-Zealand gave me a lot of experience. And trying the many local recipes I learned while traveling is an extra bonus. As it's a good way to recall excellent memories.

Gaming

While growing up gaming has been an ever-present activity in my life. First with my brothers at home, or on our annual roadtrip to Spain. The frequency of playing has dropped somewhat throughout the years. But my friends and me still play together. It's a great way to keep in touch and to meet new people. Furthermore I have always been interested by the internal workings off the games I played. It's also here that my interest in coding began, by modifying small parts of the game.
